Privacy Policy

Digital Pocket
Last updated: April 28, 2026

Digital Pocket is designed to save copied text, selected file items, and pictures locally on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what the app stores, how the custom keyboard uses Full Access, and how you can delete your data.

Data Stored on Your Device

Digital Pocket stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Copied text clips saved from the clipboard or from the Digital Pocket keyboard.
  • Pinned and recent item status for clips, files, and pictures.
  • Selected file names, file paths, secure file bookmarks, and local file copies when needed to reopen or copy selected files.
  • Pictures that you choose to import or save from the clipboard.

This app data is stored in the app container or shared app group container so the main app and keyboard extension can access the saved items.

Custom Keyboard and Full Access

Digital Pocket includes a custom keyboard extension. The keyboard may request Full Access so it can save copied text into Digital Pocket's shared local app storage.

Full Access is used for this app functionality only. Digital Pocket does not use Full Access to transmit your keyboard input, copied text, files, or pictures to the developer or to third parties.

No Server Uploads

Digital Pocket does not upload your saved clips, files, pictures, keyboard input, or clipboard content to our servers. The app does not require an account.

Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Digital Pocket does not use third-party tracking, advertising SDKs, or analytics services. Digital Pocket does not sell or share your saved content with third parties.

Apple App Store Privacy

Apple defines data collection for App Store privacy labels as transmitting data off the device in a way that allows the developer or third parties to access it for longer than needed to service a real-time request. Digital Pocket's saved app data is processed and stored on device, and is not collected by the developer through the app.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete saved clips, file items, and pictures inside Digital Pocket using the delete and clear actions. You can also remove all app data by deleting Digital Pocket from your device.

Support Requests

If you contact support by email, we will use the information you choose to send only to respond to your request.

Changes to This Policy

If Digital Pocket's data practices change, this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy answers will be updated.
