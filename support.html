Keyboard Setup

  • Open Settings on your iPhone.
  • Go to General > Keyboard > Keyboards > Add New Keyboard.
  • Select Digital Pocket Keyboard.
  • Tap Digital Pocket Keyboard again and enable Allow Full Access so clips can be saved into the app.

Deleting Saved Data

You can delete saved clips, file items, and pictures directly inside the app. You can also remove all saved data by deleting Digital Pocket from your device.
